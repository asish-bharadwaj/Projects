About Us

Sonicsphere is a music review and rating website created by Kandi Jayanth and Asish Bharadwaj from the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad.

Our mission is to provide a platform for music enthusiasts to discover and share their favorite songs, artists, and albums with others, while also providing informative reviews and ratings to help guide their musical journey.
